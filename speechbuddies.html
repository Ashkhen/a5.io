Articulate Tech. speechbuddies

Overview

Articulate Technologies' main product is Speech Buddies, which are speech therapy tools. Information about tools as well as various speech problems can be found on the speechbuddies.com website.

Goal

Design a simple, interactive, and educational solution to capture and engage users, and communicate information on speech disorders.

Process

Stakeholder interviews. Analysis of current online diagnostics technology. User research to create personas and user flows. Wireframes and prototypes. Usability testing.

Tools Used

Illustrator | Keynote | Adobe Muse

RESEARCH
2 week team project in collaboration with Theo Franca. We created a prototype for a simple diagnosis tool to help parents research speech challenges and seek appropriate help. We conducted stakeholders to scope out the project, then reviewed google analytics data to create personas and user scenarios.

CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT
Synthesizing research insights, here is a user flow highlighting the path a user would take to diagnose a speech challenge.

TESTING
Changes were made to the prototype after 3 rounds of usability testing, conducted by both online and in-person contextual inquiries.
