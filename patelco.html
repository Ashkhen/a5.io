Patelco Mobile App

Overview

Patelco was founded in 1936 to serve the financial interests of employees of a particular organization. Today, they are amongst the largest credit unions in the nation.

Goal

Redesign current app to simplify the tasks of depositing checks, transferring funds, and checking account balances. Give the app a facelift and by doing so, influence the current web design.

Process

Online surveys, contextual inquiries, and user interviews to aid in persona and user flow development.
Competitive analysis and affinity diagram to determine key feature list.

Tools Used

Axure | Illustrator | Proto.io | Photoshop

RESEARCH
2 week group project with Tamara Solorzano and Ross Fischer. We designed a mobile app that allows for a simple mobile banking experience. We kicked off the project by looking at competing products in the market to identify key features. To demonstrate the competitive findings, I created this simple infographic, including app store and google play ratings.

CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT
Different navigation option were explored. Bottom screen tab bar navigation was chosen for its simplicity and optimal thumb reach on mobile devices.

TESTING
After we tested the first iteration, usability testing feedback was leveraged to create multiple iterations of the prototype. Here is the final version of the prototype I created using proto.io, incorporating flat design elements for a more modern UI and improved user experience, designed for the iPhone 5 high resolution retina display.
