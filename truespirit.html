TrueSpirit ecommerce company

Overview

True Spirit offers updated uniform fashions that comply with school dress codes, and provides product recommendations allowed by school requirements.

Goal

Design an ecommerce website with features and design elements to reflect TrueSpiritâ€™s fresh and modern ideology.

Process

Competitive analysis of existing school uniform sites. Card sorting method to establish IA. User flows and sitemap. Wireframes. Prototypes. Usability Testing.

Tools Used

Omnigraffle | Photoshop | InVision

RESEARCH
In this 2 week project, I designed a website to fit TrueSpirit's brand ideology, incorporating all key features that I discovered through market research and card sorting method of product categories.

CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT
These user flows demonstrate 2 user journeys, for public and registered users. I created these flows based on the insights gained by studying persona types.

TESTING
